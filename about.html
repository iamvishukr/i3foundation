About Us

Discover our vision, mission, and the impact we aim to make.

Our Vision

A world where every individual is equipped with the skills, confidence, and creativity to lead and innovate across diverse fields.

Our Mission

To empower and inspire 1.0 million youths through comprehensive programs in STEM, Adventure Sports, and Fine Arts & Culture, fostering holistic development and societal contribution by 2030.

Our Objective

To implement innovative educational initiatives, adventurous activities, and cultural programs that enhance personal growth, encourage teamwork, and celebrate diversity.

Our Goal

To provide transformative experiences and opportunities that nurture talent, build character, and promote cultural appreciation among youth.

Founder

Mr. Rajeev Gupta, the Founder and Director of i3 Foundation, brings a wealth of experience and leadership across various domains. With over 29 years in the Commercial Taxes Department, he served as an officer handling VAT transactions and IT operations. His tenure included pioneering roles such as the first Treasury Officer, overseeing government budgets, TDS, and salary functions. After a distinguished career, he transitioned to corporate relations at JSW Steel Ltd. as DGM and later contributed significantly as Principal Consultant at PWC India.

Mr. Rajeev Gupta is also recognized for his impactful initiatives, including curating and licensing TEDx Kanke Ranchi, co-founding Ranchi Mall, and establishing the Jharkhand Open Coffee Club (JOCC). His expertise was pivotal in the transition to GST in Jharkhand, where he served as team lead. His career reflects a commitment to innovation, community engagement, and transformative leadership, essential foundations he brings to the i3 Foundation.
